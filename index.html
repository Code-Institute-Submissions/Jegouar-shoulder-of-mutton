THE SHOULDER OF MUTTON is a CAMRA award-winning corner pub, pleasantly perched in the centre of Wantage in Oxfordshire.

We sell craft beer, offer accommodation and host social events.

Searching for a good atmosphere? You've found it.

Here at the pub, we're all about beer and people.

Our speciality is creating the ideal place...

...for you to enjoy skillfully served real ales.

A famously quirky three sided bar, an open fireplace...

...and a comfy snug all help make it a welcoming place.

Of course, the real feel is down to our customers.

We are proud to be home to a whole host of characters...

...artists, brewers, musicians, scientists and craftsmen.

You're certain to find a great conversation.
