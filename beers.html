Are you a fan of real ale like us?

We have ten hand pumps that feature a wide selection of constantly changing real ales.

We take great pleasure in maintaining a well balanced choice of cask ales, ranging from traditional bitters, milds and stouts to contemporary, hoppy IPAs and unfined or experimental beers.

Whether you're an ale expert or a craft beer novice, our aim is to cater to all tastes.

Where do we get the beer?

A mixture of our favourite breweries from around the United Kingdom such as:

  • Butts from Great Shefford
  • Vibrant Forest from Hardley
  • Dark Star from Partridge Green
  • Tiny Rebel from Newport
  • XT from Long Crendon
  • Siren Craft from Finchampstead
  • Thornbridge from Bakewell
  • Salopian from Shrewsbury
  • Oakham from Peterborough
  • Mallinsons from Huddersfield
  • Tap Social from Oxford
  • Ilkley from West Yorkshire
  • Great Heck from North Yorkshire
  • Loddon from Dunsden
